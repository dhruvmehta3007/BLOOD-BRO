What is this all about ?


We solve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting
blood donors directly with people in blood need.

What we do ?

We facilitate the direct connection between blood donors and recipients, eliminating the need for intermediaries like blood banks, to ensure a streamlined and effective process.


Innovative

Blood Bro presents a groundbreaking solution to tackle worldwide healthcare challenges by offering prompt access to blood donors.

Network

Blood Bro is part of a collaborative network of community organizations that work together to promptly respond to emergencies in a highly efficient manner.

Get notified

Blood Bro Connect collaborates with network partners to establish connections between blood donors and recipients using an automated SMS service .

Totally Free

Blood Bro aims to offer a user-friendly, easily accessible, quick, effective, and dependable method for obtaining life-saving blood at absolutely no charge.

Save Life

We are a non profit foundation and our main objective is to make sure that everything is done to protect vulnerable persons.

Volunteers

Volunteers are essential to us! 96% of our whole staff is comprised of volunteers, who enable us to continue our humanitarian work. Giving blood is beneficial.,
 Our volunteers are ready to assist and provide blood donations around-the-clock. Blood banks save bags of blood, but in an emergency, our volunteers are available to provide a real-time blood transfusion to you.
